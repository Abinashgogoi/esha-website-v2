Access Grants

Bridging the gap between curiosity and capability. Innovation, Research, and Learning Support for Assam.

1. Introduction

At ESHA Hub, we believe that innovation and research should not be limited by a lack of resources or opportunities. An Access Grant is our mechanism to open doors for learners, creators, and researchers across Assam and beyond.

Unlike traditional grants that focus primarily on monetary disbursement, the ESHA Hub Access Grant focuses on providing access to the ecosystem. It is an authorization and support system designed to remove barriers for individuals who demonstrate a genuine willingness to learn, build, and solve problems.

2. Purpose and Philosophy

The core philosophy of ESHA Hub is that human potential is defined by skill, ethics, and intent, not by age or background. We serve a community ranging from school children to senior citizens.

Enable Learning

Deep dive into subjects without commercial pressure.

Support Research

Test theoretical knowledge in a practical environment.

Foster Innovation

Reduce friction for starting new projects with tools & guidance.

4. Eligibility & Evaluation

Who can apply?

  • Learners of any age (Children, Youth, Adults, Seniors).
  • Residents of Assam and the broader community.
  • Individuals aligning with our ethical values.

Evaluation Criteria

  • Intent & Curiosity: Desire to learn/solve.
  • Effort: Evidence of self-learning.
  • Ethics: Responsible innovation.
  • Clear Purpose: Well-defined need for access.

6. Availability

ESHA Hub operates with finite resources. Grants are resource-dependent and subject to phased releases. We may limit active slots to ensure quality support.

7. Responsibility

Recipients become stewards of our resources. We expect transparent usage, community respect, and zero tolerance for misuse or unethical behavior.

8. Future Development

As ESHA Hub grows, the Access Grant will evolve. We encourage the community to view this as a living program that adapts to the needs of Assam‚Äôs innovation ecosystem.

9. Disclaimer

No Guarantees: All information provided reflects current policies. ESHA Hub does not guarantee that every applicant will receive a grant, nor that a grant will lead to commercial success. ESHA Hub reserves the right to modify or terminate the program without prior notice.
